Connecting people Worldwide

Why ejob

E-job differentiates itself by providing a constructive platform that will set the industry standard in the blockchain world for connecting an individual in search of a service with an individual or organization providing that particular service. E-job will allow virtually any service to be advertised on its platform, users on one end will be able to search for services they need on a local and global scale and vice versa, service providers can advertise on a local or global scale. The best part about everything?

E-job is completely decentralized, there’s not any room for fake reviews, fake ratings, late payment to service providers, or getting scammed on a service you payed for. E-job will utilize smart-contracts to ensure the work or service payed for is completed and will hold funds until all negotiated terms of both parties are met. E-job is here to change the way business is done with blockchain!

Current Market

High Fee’s

If you search the internet for “Freelancing sites”, you’ll find many. What’s the one thing they all have in common? – The ridiculous fees they charge to both the client AND Freelancer. These fees are usually around 20% for the freelancer, and 5-10% also for the client.

Online only

In today’s times, the majority of freelancing sites limit their services to jobs that can only be completed online. How is E-job different in this area? E-job connects demand for offline services with local supply, and this includes basically anything you can think of.

No crypto

It is still especially rare to find freelancing service sites that accept payment other than Credit Card, PayPal, E-check, Bank Transfer, etc. How is E-job different in this area? E-job not only accepts the above listed ways to pay, but also multiple types of cryptocurrency payments: BTC, ETH, LTC, and E-job tokens.

If you’ve ever heard the term “All in one”, E-job is just that.

Ejob will support online & offline jobs

Whether you’re a freelance application developer, graphic-designer, or video-editor promoting your work on E-job, a mechanic, plumber, or landscaper… there are no limits to what can be hosted on the E-job platform and all lines of work are encouraged to use our application. Obviously, someone looking for a plumber must have a pretty urgent need, this is why E-job will use one’s location to directly cater the best services nearby to the consumer in need. On a different note, a consumer looking for a website designer could indeed use E-job to find somebody local, however a particular service such as this one could be outsourced to any E-job website designer in the entire world. We give the consumer the option to connect with a service provider locally or globally based on their individual necessities.

Meet Olly

The EJOB AI

Olly is smart

Olly is E-job’s useful AI assistant that will utilize specific user information to determine the best search results for each individual. AI technology is absolutely incredible, and we believe E-job users will be fascinated over Olly’s ability to use specific data points from previous search queries to cater unique search results for every single E-job user. The AI will be able to match a service provider as the 1st search result over another service provider of the same trade based on characteristics of both the consumer and the service provider. Hundreds of distinct data points will be used by Olly to enable these features. We’re not interested in showing hundreds of choices, we’re interested in showing the perfect result and nothing less.

Olly is smart
